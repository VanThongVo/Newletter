Guidelines and measures to prevent Corona virus (COVID-19) at PwC Vietnam

Effective 17 February 2020
Due to the spread of novel coronavirus (Covid-19), which was declared a public health emergency by the World Health Organisation on 30 January 2020, PwC Vietnam (PwC) has implemented safeguards and taken additional steps to minimise its potential impact on our clients, employees and guests.

We have:

  • Imposed restricted business travel to China, Hong Kong SAR, Macau SAR, South Korea, Iran, Japan, Schengen area and the UK;
  • Encouraged a heightened awareness of personal hygiene including the use of masks and hand sanitisers as appropriate;
  • Directed our people to leverage the use of technology for the conduct of internal events and/or meetings to the extent possible.
